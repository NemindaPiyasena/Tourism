OPEN GROUPS

This is a fantastic option if you are traveling alone, as a couple or in a small group. “Open Group” trips occur on fixed dates, are a reasonable option for individuals than “Private Guiding” packages, have a capacity of 4-6 people, and includes transportation. These trips are typically made up of intermediate to advanced level divers with good experience under their belts – people who appreciate all the benefits of traveling with a professional guide and love to dive under the blue ocean all day, but don’t have the desire to push into super technical diving terrain. You get to choose the location from the given list. It’s a fun opportunity to meet other “like-minded” divers.

The insurance giant Sri Lanka Insurance sets new standard for the travel insurance market providing exclusive benefits for travellers with the re-launching of Sri Lanka Insurance ‘Travel Protect’ insurance policy. For the first time in Sri Lanka travellers can now obtain travel insurance without a medical certificate up to 80 years of age. Also the traveller’s home will be protected against natural disasters, fire, lightning, etc. Among the benefits on offer are covers for unforeseen medical emergencies, delay or cancellation of flights , insurance against loss of personal properties and home safety while travel around the globe with ease of their mind. Also, ‘Travel Protect’ provides 24 hour travel and medical assistance by partnering with renowned service providers to ensure safety of travellers anywhere in the world. Also when travelling with parents, children below the age of 18 years can get 50% off from the premium.Travel Protect’ provides coverage for individuals, families, and business professionals and also it provides special benefits for frequent travellers who travel multiple times during a year. In order to meet the different needs of travellers ‘Travel Protect’ offers seven travel schemes under Silver, Gold, Platinum and Asia 25 packages.

SKI & SAIL IN Patna

Patna Ski Lodge with sail and steel keel! Our cooperation with S/Y SkyDancer is like a floating Ski Lodge. Wake up in the morning to the smell of coffee and a view of your next ski objective. With the flexibility of a boat we can go to places hardly seen by any skiers and seek out the best water available. Ski & sail requires a highly trained guide to adjust the itinerary to the current conditions and weather. For this reason we only use IFMGA certified ski guides who have local knowledge of the area. This way we can offer a flexible program, with the best knowledge, as well as ensuring safety. Our captain and crew on the boat is trained by the RYA in England, have good local knowledge and extensive international experience to ensure a sailing trip you will not forget.
We offer ski & sail programs in Lofoten in February and March.
In April we go to South Patna with our ski & sail program before we sail to Svalbard and end the ski & sail program in June. Visit Northern Alpine Guides at Ski & Sail to read more about ski & sail programs.

This package offers you the perfect solution – our local surf guides share their knowledge with you, taking you to the best waves and giving you top tips to find secret spots with no crowds During a typical 7 night stay with us, we will show you beautiful beach and reef breaks like Devils Rock, Anza, Anchor Point and more.
